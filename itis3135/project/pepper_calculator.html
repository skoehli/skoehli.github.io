Don't Know How Many Peppers You Need For a Batch?

Making your own hot sauce can be tricky. You want a good balance of flavor and heat - but this balance can be hard to find, especially if you're just starting out.

Luckily, we have developed this tool to help aspiring sauce connoisseurs find a good starting point in terms of heat and flavor while having enough peppers to make their batch of sauces.
